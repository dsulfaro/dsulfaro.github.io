My Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of cream cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cups white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • I premade graham cracker pie crust

Description

This is the easiest cheesecake recipe ever. It's quick, versatile, cheap, and quite tasty!

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Make the filling by mixing together all of the filling ingredients till smooth. Use a mixer set at low-medium speed.
  3. Pour the filling into the crust.
  4. Bake it for 20 minutes, then add a crust shield; or shield the crust with strips of aluminum foil. Bake for an additional 10 minutes (for a total of about 30 minutes)
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the oven. Place in fridge until set (about 4 hours or best overnight)
  6. Serve with berries or topping of choice.
